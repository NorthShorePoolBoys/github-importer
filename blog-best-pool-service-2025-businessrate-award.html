Our first regional award — and it came from our customers

The BusinessRate Best of 2025 Award wasn't something we applied for. It's not a pay-to-play badge or a directory listing. BusinessRate analyzes verified Google Reviews across every pool cleaning service in Southern New England — Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and Connecticut — and awards the top-rated business in each category.

In July 2025, that top spot belonged to North Shore Pool Boys. A small, CPO-certified team based in Peabody, MA — beating out every pool service company across three states. Not because of our marketing budget, but because of what our customers wrote about their experience working with us.

What our customers said

The reviews that powered this award consistently highlight the same things: Chris returns every call. The team shows up on time. Pricing is transparent with no surprises. The work is thorough and professional. Problems are diagnosed honestly — not manufactured to sell unnecessary repairs.

"After dealing with three different pool companies who dropped the ball, I finally found North Shore Pool Boys and I won't be going anywhere else. Their communication was excellent, they showed up on time, were completely transparent about pricing." — Boston Alex, Google Review

That review, and dozens like it, are what earned this recognition. Every five-star rating from a homeowner in Danvers, every detailed review from a family in Beverly, every referral from a neighbor in Salem — they all added up to something bigger than we expected.

What we offer across the North Shore

The award covers our full range of pool and hot tub services: weekly maintenance, seasonal openings and closings, equipment repair, water chemistry, liner installation, heater installation, leak detection, trenchless pipe repair, and hot tub service.

We serve 16 towns from our base in Peabody: Danvers, Middleton, Beverly, Salem, Saugus, Lynnfield, Marblehead, Swampscott, Lynn, Wakefield, Reading, North Reading, Topsfield, Wenham, and Hamilton.

The 2025 award motivated us to keep raising the bar. In 2026, we earned the BusinessRate Benchmark Award — their highest recognition for maintaining top-rated status over consecutive years. But it all started here, with this award, and with the customers who made it possible.
