### Backlink Opportunities (Beyond Directories)

1. **BusinessRate award page** — You already won their award. Make sure their listing links to your site (it likely does). Request a badge/backlink if not.
2. **Local news coverage** — Submit a press release about back-to-back award wins to Patch.com (Peabody, Danvers, Salem editions), Wicked Local, and Salem News.
3. **Pool supply companies** — If you buy from specific suppliers, ask if they have a "find a pro" or dealer locator you can be listed on.
4. **Real estate agents** — Offer to be their recommended pool company. Many agent sites have resource pages that link to local service providers.
5. **Town websites** — Some North Shore town sites have local business directories. Check Peabody, Danvers, and Beverly municipal sites.
6. **Sponsorships** — Sponsor a local Little League team, school event, or community pool. These often come with a website backlink.
