Our Approach

We find the root cause — not just the obvious symptom

Hot tub problems are often interconnected. A "heater issue" might actually be a flow switch problem. Weak jets could be a clogged filter, a failing pump, or an air lock in the plumbing. We don't guess — we systematically test each component to find the real cause.

Once we know what's wrong, we give you an honest recommendation. If a $50 part fixes the problem, that's what we'll tell you. We don't push unnecessary replacements to inflate the bill.

We service all major hot tub brands including Jacuzzi, Hot Spring, Sundance, Bullfrog, Caldera, and more.

Common Repairs

What we fix most often

⚙️

Pump Repair & Replacement

Circulation pumps, jet pumps, and booster pumps. We repair seals, bearings, and impellers or replace the full unit when needed.

🔥

Heater Troubleshooting

Won't heat, overheating, or error codes? We diagnose heating elements, thermostats, flow switches, and pressure sensors.

💨

Jet & Air System Repair

Weak jets, stuck jets, or no air. We fix diverter valves, air controls, jet body replacements, and air lock issues.

🖥️

Control Panel & Electrical

Error codes, unresponsive controls, GFCI tripping, and wiring issues. We diagnose and repair or replace control boards and sensors.

💧

Plumbing & Leaks

Visible leaks, water loss, and wet equipment areas. We locate the leak source and repair unions, manifolds, and pipe connections.

🛡️

Cover & Shell Repair

Waterlogged covers, cracked shells, broken hinges, and damaged cover lifters. We repair or replace to keep your spa insulated and protected.
